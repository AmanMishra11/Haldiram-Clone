Explore Our Haldiram Namkeen List: A Diverse Range of Namkeens


We have a wide range of namkeens that people just love throughout India; however, we have listed some of the best options below:


  • Bhujia: Crunchy, spicy, and finger-licking, Bhujia can never go out of season whatsoever, on any occasion. This is one of the best namkeens that has remained a favorite across generations.
  • Salted Peanut: Enjoy the classic taste of crunchy peanuts, all perfectly seasoned. The perfect snack any time.
  • Panchrattan: A majestic blend of five different flavors combined to explode in every single bite.
  • Moong Dal: A fried split gram light mixture snack, seasoned with a pinch of salt for delightful crunchiness.
  • Navrattan: This is a Namkeen from South India, mixed with different ingredients. Every mouthful is just perfect.
  • Khatta Meetha: Sweet and sour mixture namkeen, which will make a subtle exodus of taste buds with every nibble.
