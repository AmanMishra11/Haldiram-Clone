Get Delicious Healthy Snacks Only at Haldiram’s

Are you looking for healthy snacks at best prices? Check out Haldiram’s- your ultimate destination for healthy savoury snacks- the perfect delight for your taste buds without compromising on nutrition. Whether you are looking for healthy evening snacks to relish after a long day, healthy snacks for kids that are tasty and nutritious, or high-protein snacks for your active lifestyle, we have something for everyone.

Flavors in Every Bite

At Haldiram's, we understand that tea time is sacrosanct and hence have put together a broad array of snacks well-suited for your cup of chai. A variety of Chai Time Snacks includes traditional items that have been loved by generations. From the crunchiness of Mathri to the spiciness of Fatafat Bhel, our snacks are cut out to perfection, ensuring every bite is as fulfilling as the last one

Popular Products from Our Extensive Range

  • Kachori: Crispy and fluffy, with a spicy mixture of lentils inside, this kachori is an absolute delight for any chai lover. Having just the right spice for the perfect taste, this could be an Indian snack that you simply cannot resist when served with your cup of tea.
  • Samosa Small: These small, crispy pockets are infused with flavorful potato filling, deep-frying them to golden perfection. Have a taste of the traditional samosas in a size that's just perfect for munching.
  • Nimki Suwali: Light and crunchy, nimki suwalli has a pretty mild taste; it is the perfect snack that everyone craves. The perfect healthy snack with chai, just for those who want something light.
  • Mathri: The classic favorite of all time, Mathri is a savory snack in a flaky avatar, seasoned with spices, and deep-fried to perfection. Just pair it up with chai for a classic tea time snack.
  • Fatafat Bhel: Spicy, sour, crunchy-all the ingredients combine in a delicious mouthful for spice lovers. This is a perfect Indian snack when enjoyed with tea, raising spirits when you feel something spicy.
