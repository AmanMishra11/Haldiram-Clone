Enjoy the Finest Indian Sweets with Haldiram's Mithai


With Haldiram's Mithai, taste the timeless flavors of India where tradition embraces taste with every single bite.


At Haldiram's, we pride ourselves on crafting diverse authentic Indian sweets, each crafted with an effort to give you the richest and most satisfying experience. Whether it be for some special occasion or just a sweet craving, our varieties of mithai guarantee a mouthwatering journey into the best Indian confectionery.





Our Popular Mithai Options



Pista Burfi: Made with just the right amount of pistachios and khoya, it simply melts in your mouth, leaving behind the nutty flavor that's hard to avoid.



Mysore Pak: Rich, decadent, and made with gram flour, ghee and sugar, this classic South Indian sweet greets one with a crumbly texture that bursts with flavor.



Soan Cake: Soft and juicier, our invention, the soan cake, of traditional soan papdi allows for the right mix of sweetness and texture.



Mathura Peda: Made from condensed milk and clarified butter, this sweet originates from the holy city of Mathura. A pinch of cardamom adds to the irresistible charm of this sweet.



Karachi Halwa: A jelly-like temptation made with corn flour and sugar mixed with ghee, dotted with nuts, adding a crunch to every bite.



Milk Cake: Traditional Indian delicacy loaded with milk and cooked on low heat till it reaches perfection-this cake delivers a rich, satisfying, dense, caramelized flavor.
